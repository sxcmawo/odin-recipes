Banana Oatmeal Muffins

Healthy Banana Oatmeal Muffins

These healthy banana oatmeal muffins are a healthy breakfast idea you can make ahead and take on the go! Perfect healthy breakfast muffins for kids too!

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 1.5 cup oats
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp yogurt
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350F/180C.
  2. Mash one banana and whisk with the egg, yogurt and coconut oil.
  3. Add the vanilla and baking powder.
  4. Add the oats and chop 1/2 banana in chunks - add these to the mixtures too.
  5. Pour the batter into silicone muffin cups and top with a slice of banana (optional).
  6. Bake the muffins for 20-30 minutes, checking on them every now and then. The muffins are done when they're nicely brown and a toothpick comes out clean when you punch them with it.
  7. Let cool off and enjoy!
