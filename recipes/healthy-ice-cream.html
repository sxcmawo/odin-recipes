3-ingredient Healthy Ice-Cream

You'll love this refreshing, 3-ingredient healthy ice cream recipe that requires no machine! This strawberry ice cream recipe will become your favorite summer dessert in no time!

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cup strawberries
  • 1 small banana
  • 1/2 cup milk of choice

Steps

  1. Use frozen banana and frozen strawberries. Add all ingredients to your high speed blender or let the fruit defrost for a bit and blend with an immersion blender. Blend until there are no chunks of fruit left. I needed to open the blender, scoop and blend again to get everyhing blended.
  2. You can experiment add less milk to make the ice cream thicker, but this is perfect for serving immediately.
  3. Top with your favorite toppings and enjoy!
