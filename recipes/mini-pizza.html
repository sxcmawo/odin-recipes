Mini Pizza

Please a crowd with these fun mini pizzas made with sliced rolls and topped with pineapple, pepperoni, and basil.

Ingredients

  • 6 each King's Hawaiian Rolls
  • 1 (14 ounce) jar pizza sauce
  • 1.5 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup pineapple, chopped
  • 1 cup pepperoni, chopped
  • 6 leaves fresh basil, chopped
  • 1 pinch Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Slice Kings Hawaiian Rolls in half and flatten to about 1/2-inch thick. Place slices face up on baking sheet. Spread pizza sauce on each slice. Top bread slices with shredded cheese, pineapple and pepperoni.
  3. Cook till cheese is lightly browned, approximately 7 to 8 minutes.
